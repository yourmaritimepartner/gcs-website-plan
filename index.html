Executive Summary

This document outlines the strategic plan for relaunching the gcship.com website to support Gulf County Shipbuilding's revitalization initiative. The website must serve two distinct audiences:

  • Federal contracting officers (USCG, Navy)
  • Commercial B2B buyers (barge operators, infrastructure companies)
"To serve as the Gulf Coast's precision engineering hub, dedicated to the construction and sustainment of American vessels essential for national security and the growth of domestic maritime trade."

Content Writing Guidelines

Voice & Tone

  • Professional but not stuffy
  • Confident but not boastful
  • Technical where appropriate
  • Patriotic undertones (national security, American jobs)

Compliance Language Rules

DO use:
  • "Actively pursuing [certification]"
  • "Compliance program underway"
  • "[System] implemented"
  • "Committed to achieving"
DON'T use:
  • Claims of certifications not yet achieved
  • "Certified" or "Compliant" without verification
  • Vague or misleading status descriptions

Stakeholder Review Questions

Please provide feedback on the following:

  1. Mission Statement: Does the proposed mission statement accurately reflect GCS's vision?
  2. Page Priority: Do you agree with the phased approach and page priorities?
  3. Compliance Language: Are the suggested status descriptions accurate for current certifications?
  4. Photography: What existing photos/assets are available? What needs to be scheduled?
  5. Leadership: Who should be featured on the Leadership page at launch?
  6. Case Studies: Are there historical projects we can feature?
  7. Technical Platform: Any preferences or existing relationships with web platforms?
  8. Timeline: Is the 6-week target realistic given current resources?
